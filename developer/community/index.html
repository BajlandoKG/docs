Developers Community

We have a vibrant developers’ community at vanillaforums.org. Please register there to ask general questions, contribute feedback and ideas, help others, add your own plugins & themes to the official directory, and sign the contributors’ agreement.

Get Notified

Want to get notified about the newest releases?

  • Sign up for the VanillaForums.org newsletter in your community profile.
  • Watch the RSS feed for the ‘blog’ category.
  • Follow our Twitter account.
